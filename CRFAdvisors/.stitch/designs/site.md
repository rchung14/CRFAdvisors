- **Service Detail — Loan Review**
  H2: `Independent Loan Review for Banks and Financial Institutions`
  Content: Independent assessment of loan portfolio quality. Evaluates credit underwriting standards, risk rating accuracy, loan classification, and compliance with regulatory guidelines. Identifies potential problem credits early. Serves national and community banks, FCUs, savings institutions.
  Keywords: independent loan review, credit review banks, loan portfolio review, risk rating validation

- **Service Detail — Due Diligence**
  H2: `Credit Due Diligence for Mergers, Acquisitions, and Transactions`
  Content: Comprehensive credit due diligence for M&A activity, whole bank acquisitions, and portfolio purchases. Evaluates credit quality, concentrations, underwriting standards, and reserve adequacy prior to transaction close.
  Keywords: credit due diligence, bank M&A credit review, loan portfolio due diligence

- **Service Detail — Portfolio Stress Testing**
  H2: `Loan Portfolio Stress Testing for Regulatory Compliance`
  Content: Scenario-based stress testing of commercial, residential, and consumer loan portfolios. Models economic downturn scenarios, credit loss projections, and capital impact for regulatory and internal planning purposes.
  Keywords: portfolio stress testing, loan stress test, bank stress test model, credit loss projection

- **Service Detail — ALLL Methodology & Review**
  H2: `ALLL Methodology Review and FFIEC Compliance`
  Content: Review and implementation of Allowance for Loan and Lease Losses (ALLL) methodology. Ensures compliance with FFIEC Interagency Policy Statement and GAAP. Includes impaired loan valuation, TDR accounting, and OREO review.
  Keywords: ALLL methodology, allowance for loan losses, FFIEC compliance, impaired loan valuation, TDR accounting

- **Service Detail — CECL Model & Implementation**
  H2: `CECL Model Development and Implementation for Financial Institutions`
  Content: End-to-end support for Current Expected Credit Loss (CECL) model development, validation, and implementation. Tailored to institution size and portfolio complexity. Includes data gap analysis, model selection, parallel run support, and documentation for regulatory review.
  Keywords: CECL model implementation, CECL consulting, current expected credit loss, CECL for community banks, CECL for credit unions
